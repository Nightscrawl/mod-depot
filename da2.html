Dragon Age II Mods

Invisible Unequipped Weapons

As with my DAI mod of the same name, this makes all of the default weapons, seen even when you have unequipped your standard weapons, invisible. You are still able to attack things, you just can't see the weapons.

Get it on the Nexus.

Fenris Markings Redux

After years of putting up with Fenris's chalky markings and not being entirely satisfied with any of the Nexus versions I finally decided to do my own fix. These are drawn from scratch with some minor, hardly visible elements changed because I couldn't see a decent workaround for the problems I was having.

Get it on the Nexus.

A Brighter Hightown

This is a very simple retexture of just three Hightown textures.

With the two exterior textures I aimed to brighten the stone and reduce the amount of moss which made it look quite grimy. Keep in mind that Kirkwall is over 1000 years old, the stone should have wear and show age, but that doesn't mean it has to be dirty. This is where the Kirkwall aristocracy live, I'm sure that it has the benefit of better maintenance than the default textures lead us to believe.

The third texture is a recolor of the interior Hightown floor tile to be similar to the loading screen art with a gray stone and golden grouting.

Get it on the Nexus.

Merrill Hair Options

I can't stand Merrill's hairstyle, even when on other NPCs. Almost all of the Merrill mods here on the Nexus also change her face in some way, which I didn't want. Here are five different options to choose from. These are default female elf hairs in the game, no other mods needed.

Get it on the Nexus.
